Maroon 5

Maroon 5 is an American pop rock band that originated in Los Angeles, California. The band was formed in 2001 and consists of lead vocalist Adam Levine, guitarist James Valentine, bassist Mickey Madden, keyboardist Jesse Carmichael, and drummer Matt Flynn. Maroon 5 is known for their catchy melodies, soulful vocals, and a blend of pop, rock, and R&B influences.
