Warranty & Replacement

Reliable Warranty. Hassle-Free Replacement.

Warranty Coverage

Accessories

(Keyboard, Mouse, Cables, Headsets, Adapters)

6-12 months manufacturer warranty (depends on brand)

Electronics

(Monitors, Speakers, Routers, Webcam, External Storage)

1-2 years manufacturer warranty

Premium Brands

(Logitech, Razer, Asus, MSI, HyperX, etc.)

  • 1-2 years local or international warranty
  • Some products offer On-Site Warranty

PC Components

(SSD, RAM, PSU, GPU, CPU)

3-10 years (depending on brand/model)

What's Covered

Manufacturing Defects

Keys not working

USB connection failure

Screen dead pixels or lines

SSD not detected

Hardware Malfunction

Device cannot power on

Unexpected shutdown

Audio or sensor failure

Internal Component Faults

Motherboard defects

Power regulation issues

Internal board malfunction

*What's NOT Covered

Warranty does not apply to:

Human Damage

Drops, impact damage

Water damage or moisture

Cracked parts

Burn marks from misuse

Unauthorized Repair

Opening or modifying the device

Removed screws/security seals

Cosmetic Issues

Scratches

Stains or discoloration

External aging

Consumable Items

Mouse feet

Key caps (depends on brand)

Grip pads

Cables (unless DOA)

Replacement Policy

DOA Replacement

(Dead on Arrival - 1-to-1 Exchange)

If the product fails within 7 days upon receiving, we offer a 1-to-1 replacement.


Eligible conditions:

  • Cannot power on
  • Serious manufacturer defect
  • Functions not working out of the box

Must provide:

  • Product photos/videos
  • Order number
  • Full packaging & accessories

Warranty Replacement

(After 7 Days)


If the product is faulty after 7 days but within warranty:

  • Product will be sent for assessment
  • If confirmed faulty, replacement or repair will be provided

Possible outcomes:

  • New replacement
  • Manufacturer repair
  • Replacement with an equal or higher model (if discontinued)

Assessment duration: 3-14 working days

Replacement Conditions


  • Full packaging and accessories required
  • Purchase proof required
  • Serial number must match the order
  • No human damage

  • Restart device
  • Update drivers

Step3
