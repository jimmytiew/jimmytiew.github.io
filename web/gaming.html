Buying guide

How to Choose the Right Gear?
Mice Buying Tips
  • DPI & Sensor quality
  • Weight matters
  • Shape & Grip Type (Palm / Claw / Finger-tip)
  • Wireless vs Wired
  • Buttons & Scroll Wheel quality
Keyboard Buying Tips
  • Choose your switch type: Linear / Tactile / Clicky
  • Pick your layout: 60%, 65%, TKL, Full Size
  • Consider features: RGB, Hot-Swappable, Wireless
  • Build Quality: Material, weight, sound profile
Controller Buying Tips
  • Compatibility with PC / PS / Xbox
  • Trigger sensitivity & analog stick quality
  • Wired vs Wireless
  • Button layout & customization
Headset Buying Tips
  • Sound quality: clarity, bass, and stereo imaging
  • Microphone quality for communication
  • Comfort: ear cushion material & headband pressure
  • Virtual surround sound (7.1) support
  • Platform compatibility (PC / PS / Xbox)
Speaker Buying Tips
  • Sound quality balance (bass, mids, highs)
  • Speaker type: 2.0 / 2.1 / 5.1
  • Bass performance & need for subwoofer
  • Connectivity: Bluetooth / USB / 3.5mm / Optical
  • Desk space and speaker size
Microphone Buying Tips
  • Mic type: Condenser vs Dynamic
  • Polar pattern: Cardioid / Omni / Bidirectional
  • Noise handling & clarity
  • Mounting: desk stand, boom arm, shock mount
  • Best for your use case: streaming, recording, meetings
